My Promise of Hope

As a person who believes life will always have a meaning, hope is one of the things I always ask for from God. In many ways, I believe the truth that God has His ways of showing me the correct path. Through His love and wisdom, I am guided by Him through my prayers. To always go to mass every Sunday, and to be with God in peace.

My Promise of Faith

No matter how hard something might get, I have always learned to stand up for what is right, because I know that God is with me. As my life role is to become a doctor, one of the many things I ask myself to become is one who helps other people. The concrete action that I consider doing is to always pray before eating, to remind myself to always thank God for His sacrifices for us.
