My Promise of Hope

“Sanctify and Holiness”. As an altar server, I live out the holiness and sanctify my life through mass, to live my life in line with his teachings with Christ, as the Eternal high priest we are reminded of his sacrifice. “Humility in service”. Christ who is God became man and suffered death for us, a king without a crown but a cross. As an aspiring leader, I shall lead with humility while serving others.

My Promise of Faith

“Veritas in Caritate” or Truth in love. God’s embodiment as truth and life sustains our life. As a human being, I promise to always be reminded of the truth so that I may live like Christ did. With these aspects of Christ’s mission for the salvation of humanity, I am empowered by looking at him hanging from the cross. He who is without sin, suffered the consequences of ours. I too shall follow this. To live like Christ and be the light for others.
