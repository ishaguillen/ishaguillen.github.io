My Promise of Hope

Christ's threefold mission as Priest, King, and Prophet inspires me to live a life rooted in hope, truth, and service. As a priest, I promise to pray for others and offer my daily struggles to God as a spiritual sacrifice. As a prophet, I will speak the truth with love and stand up for what is right, even when it's difficult or when evil tempts me not to do so. As a king, I will lead through kindness and serve others humbly, especially those in need.

My Promise of Faith

I will work hard to build a more loving and inclusive community. Each day, I strive to bring the light of Christ to others by being patient, compassionate, and faithful. One concrete action I can take to live out this calling is to actively listen to someone struggling, offering them a safe space to share their burdens and reminding them of their worth and God's unwavering love.
