My Promise of Hope

As I live a life of constant battle between desire and humility, Jesus serves as a reminder that there is always hope. When I question God’s presence and my faith flickers, He stays in my heart to open our eyes to the truth, and serve others out of love. As our high priest, Jesus became my inspiration to offer my life in service to the needs of others. However, rightful service manifests itself through Jesus, who ruled with justice and mercy, thus, I promise to lead with equity and righteousness by giving my gratitude and asking for guidance from Him.

My Promise of Faith

Just like Jesus who proclaimed the word of God, I intend to show others the truth of being God’s followers — serving humanity is not solely for the purpose of having a ticket to His kingdom, but rather to motivate others to restore their faith and come back to our God. I do not have a significant title in the house of God, but I acknowledge my obligation to Him, starting from baptism and confirmation, and promise to obey Him, which is the best, genuine act I can do to uphold my calling. I promise to be a perpetual pillar of truth, love, and compassion by keeping Jesus as the center of my life.
