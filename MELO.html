My Promise of Hope

Whenever I encounter struggles and challenges, they can be quite difficult sometimes, and there are instances where I begin to question if there is hope and faith for me to use. However, I am constantly reminded of Christ and the guide He has set out for me to live a life in service for others and me. I promise to empower my calling as Priest, King, and Prophet even as I face these struggles and challenges in my everyday life.

My Promise of Hope

I will do my best and live a life of hope through simple prayer and devotion. I will strive and only acknowledge the Truth as I follow Jesus' path to what is right and treat others with equality and equity. I will continue with acts of service, such as taking care of my family and friends and taking the initiative to do something good or kind to my neighbors. I promise to stop questioning the challenges set out for me as I know that they shape me to become a better person and bring the light of Christ through the Faith and Hope I possess.
