My Promise of Hope

I believe Christ’s threefold mission as Priest, King, and Prophet gives me the strength to live a life filled with hope, service, and truth, even when things feel heavy. As an empath, I carry the emotions of others deeply in my heart, and I’ve come to realize that this isn’t a weakness; it’s a gift. Life hasn’t always been easy for me, and there are days when I feel like breaking, but I still show up with a smile on my face; that’s how much I care.

My Promise of Hope

I believe my purpose is to care for people in a world that often forgets how much that matters. I show love not just in words but through quiet, consistent actions that speak louder. As a Priest, I lift others in prayer through the way I show up for them. As a King, I lead by choosing compassion over pride. And as a Prophet, I try to be a voice of comfort and truth when people need it most. One way I live this out is by always being that safe space for others, because I know how powerful it is to be seen, heard, and loved.
